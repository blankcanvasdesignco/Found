Privacy Policy for Found

Effective Date: 20/03/2025

At Found, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Apple ID & Basic Profile Info: We collect information related to your Apple ID to enable app sign-in.
  • Images & Posts: We collect images you post on the app. These are stored temporarily and automatically deleted after expiry.
  • Activity Data: This includes your streak count, likes, views, and other activity logs.
  • Third-Party Analytics: We use Firebase and Adjust Analytics to collect data about app usage for improvement and analysis.
  • Permissions: We request access to your device's gallery, camera, and notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve app features.
  • To analyze usage and enhance user experience.
  • To ensure security and monitor activities within the app.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your data with third parties. Data is temporarily stored using Firebase services and analyzed using Adjust Analytics.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Posts are stored temporarily and deleted in batches after expiry.
  • Firebase is used to securely store and manage temporary data.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. User Rights

  • Users can request data deletion at any time by contacting us.
  • Users can request a copy of their data.

6. Children's Privacy

Found is not intended for users under the age of 12+.

7. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: admin@blankcanvasdesign.co
